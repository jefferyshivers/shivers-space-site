about

I'm a freelance composer and software developer, meandering intuitively around the blurry intersections of art and code.
As a software developer, I worked for GNU LilyPond for Google Summer of Code 2016, and will be a fellow at the Open Music Initiative summer lab in 2017.
As an artist, I have a master's degree in music from Tufts University, have held residencies at Atlantic Center for the Arts (Florida) and Listhus Artspace (Iceland), and have given formal presentations on my work at Harvard University and Fabra i Coats (Barcelona).
